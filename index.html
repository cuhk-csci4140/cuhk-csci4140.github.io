What, When, How?

Quote from course description:  ...this course emphasizes in arousing the creativity and fun in developing software projects.

CSCI4140 emphasizes in developing Web-based projects. Most students taking this course are senior ones.

  • Students have to proposed their own projects;
  • The teaching team recommends the right tools for students;
  • Projects are delivered as a demonstration at the end of the semester;

Students are very devoted in proposing their own projets. In most projects, students are using the most trendy technologies, including Node.js, Angular.js, Box2D, WebSocket, WebGL, etc.

The most important point is that the course did not teach most of the technologies; students are motivated and are learning by themselves. Every year, students are demonstrating their awesome work, and the teaching team is capturing those moments as videos.
